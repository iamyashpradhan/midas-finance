What you should know about us

Midas finance is into financial services. It majorly focuses on Indian Equity Markets. It was founded by Mr. Rushabh Shah on 4th Feb 2016. It is located in the heart of Mumbai. Founder Rushabh Shah completed his Msc. Investment & Risk Finance from University of Westminster, London, U.K.

The inception of the idea of starting Midas Finance was when Mr. Rushabh Shah completing his masters in science. Since then Mr. Shah has been looking after his family's equity portfolio. This over the years fueled his passion for this field, which took shape of Midas Finance.
